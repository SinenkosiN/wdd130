Durban Rapids

Discover thrilling river adventures along the spectacular uMngeni River. Our expert guides ensure fun, safety, and unforgettable outdoor experiences for families, teams, and adventure seekers.

History

Durban Rapids began as a small, local adventure company offering guided rafting experiences along the beautiful uMngeni River.

As demand grew, we created an online platform to make it easier for customers to explore our trips, learn about safety, and book exciting river adventures.
